Adrián Sager La Ganga

Computational Scientist and Engineer

École Polythecnique Fédérale de Lausanne

I am currently interested in methods for causal inference, explainable AI, and program inference.

During my master’s degree at EPFL, I have carried out multiple projects on applied machine learning, such as employing autoencoders to predict stellar properties at the astrophysics laboratory and exploring computer vision methods for image registration of a fly’s neuron activations in Pavan Ramdya’s Lab. Finally, during my thesis at IBM Research in Zürich, I applied NLP methods, uncertainty quantification, and explainable AI techniques to quantify chemical sustainability.

I enjoy hiking ⛰, playing the piano 🎹, watching old movies 📽, and learning both about philosophy and the newest tech!
